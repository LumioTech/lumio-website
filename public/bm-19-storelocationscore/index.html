FAQ

Common questions

What areas do you cover?

Currently we focus on the Netherlands, with coverage of all municipalities. Reports for Belgium and Germany are available on request — contact us first to confirm your area.

Where does the data come from?

We use publicly available open datasets: Statistics Netherlands (CBS), municipal open data portals, OpenStreetMap, PDOK, and public business registers. All sources are cited in your report.

How accurate are the scores?

Our scores are based on the best available open data, not real-time sensors. They give a reliable directional signal — but they complement, not replace, your own on-the-ground observation. We always recommend visiting the site at different times of day.

Can I get reports for multiple locations to compare?

Yes. If you're comparing 3 or more locations, contact us for a bundle discount. We can also produce a side-by-side comparison report.

What if I'm not satisfied?

If you feel the report didn't cover what was promised, we'll revise it or issue a full refund — no questions asked.
